# Day 1: AI/ML/DL/GenAI Learning

## Key Concepts Learned:

### 1. AI vs ML vs DL vs GenAI
- **AI**: Broad field of intelligent machines
- **ML**: Machines learn from data and improve over time
- **DL**: Neural networks with many layers (mimics human brain)
- **GenAI**: Creates new content (ChatGPT, DALL-E, etc.)

### 2. What is LLM?
- LLM = Large Language Model
- Trained on billions of words
- Predicts next word based on context
- Examples: ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini

### 3. How Transformers Work (High-Level)
1. **Tokenization**: Break text into words/tokens
2. **Embedding**: Convert words to numbers
3. **Self-Attention**: Find relationships between words
4. **Feed-Forward**: Process and understand meaning
5. **Output**: Predict next word or generate response

Key concept: Attention mechanism helps understand context.

### 4. Prompt Engineering
- Art of asking AI the right questions
- Techniques:
  - Be specific and clear
  - Give examples (few-shot prompting)
  - Specify desired format
  - Use step-by-step reasoning (chain-of-thought)
